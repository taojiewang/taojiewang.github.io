I’m a PhD candidate in philosophy at Brown University supervised by Nomy Arpaly, Jamie Dreier, Emily Kress, and Bernard Reginster. Before coming to Brown, I received my MA from University of Wisconsin - Milwaukee, my BA in philosophy from KU Leuven in Belgium, and my BA in English (Hon.) from University of Liverpool in the UK. You can find my CV here.

My name is pronounced Tau-J-Won. I was born and raised in Changsha, China.

My partner and I have a cat, Cotton, and a bunny, Ricey. They are great friends!
